There are some cool things happening with our hyperlinks! Can you spot them?

**Do This**

1. Open [index.html](index.html) and start the live preview.
2. Hover over the hyperlinks in the preview.
3. Click on a hyperlink _very slowly_.
4. Discuss with your neighbor - What do you notice? What do you wonder?

**In the [CSS file](style.css):**

5. Take a look at the [rule sets below the comments that say "CHECK ME OUT!"](style.css#L21-L33)
6. Discuss with your neighbor - What do you notice? What do you wonder?
