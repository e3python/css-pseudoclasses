Home The Platypus The Axolotl The Narwhal

Often referred to as the "unicorn of the sea," the narwhal is a medium-sized whale known for the long, spiraled tusk protruding from its head. The tusk is actually an elongated upper left canine tooth that can grow up to 10 feet long. Narwhals inhabit the cold waters of the Arctic and are a marvel of marine mammal evolution.
