At first glance, the platypus looks like it was assembled from parts of different animals; it has the bill and feet of a duck, the tail of a beaver, and the body of an otter. This unique mammal is one of the few venomous mammals, with males possessing a spur on their hind foot that can deliver a painful injection of venom. The platypus is also among the few mammals that lay eggs.
